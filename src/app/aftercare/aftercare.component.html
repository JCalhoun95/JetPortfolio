Typical Aftercare

- Wash tattoo once a day with plain soft soap hand soap.
- Use a small amount of plain unscented Lubriderm
rubbed all the way in on the tattoo a few times daily.
- For ALL tattoos be mindful of moisture.
- No long hot showers or baths where the tattoo would be submerged.
- If the tattoo is below the elbows avoid doing dishes.
- Be mindful of how often you wash your hands.

Aftercare with Saniderm

- Ideally we want the saniderm on for 3 days, but at least 24 hours.
- The saniderm will start to fill up with plasma and begin to look like a soy sauce packet.
This is what we want!
- Saniderm is keeping oxygen from reaching tattoo and therefore not creating a scab.
- You can tape a piece of paper towel to your skin at night over the bandage
to try to lesson the chance of pulling it off in your sleep
- If at any point you notice that the bandage has opened up
immediately remove it and follow the standard aftercare.
- At the end of three days you can remove the bandage and
follow standard aftercare until fully healed.
