About

Juliet Hendrix has been a professional tattooer for the last 4 years,
with a natural artistic ability from a very younge age.
Born, raised and having apprenticed right here in Cape Girardeau, MO,
with strong family roots in Southeast Missouri.
In tattooing, her style is more of a neotraditional or even "cartoony",
as she describes, with a flair and passion for color tattoos.
Juliet is a well rounded artist and student of many artistic mediums and interests,
including sketching, digital art, and welding.
